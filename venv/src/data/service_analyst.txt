ABOUT THE ROLE AND OUR TEAM:

We are hiring a Service Analyst for our US Local Marketplace team which connects over 2 million engaged couples with +50,000 local wedding vendors (e.g. Venue, DJ, Florist, etc.) across the US each year. The ideal candidate is a data-driven problem solver who enjoys digging into the numbers and studying drivers and trends. You are passionate about creating predictability, scalability and efficiency. You will lead the charge on transforming the Service organization into a data-driven business where we can measure, analyze and optimize the efficiency of our vendor experience. In this role you will work on a daily basis with various levels of the Local Sales and Service organization (representatives, managers and leaders alike). Additionally, you will collaborate with partners across FP&A, Data Science/Data Engineering, Technology and within the Local Business Operations and Local Operations Leadership teams.  


RESPONSIBILITIES

Provide daily reporting and dashboard packages to illustrate key vendor trends and produce actionable insights 
Benchmark and track against daily, monthly, weekly, monthly and annual performance targets and KPIs
Identify strategic areas of opportunity to improve the vendor experience through thorough analysis of feature/vendor usage patterns, maturity scores, NPS results, VoC feedback, customer experience metrics (i.e.  call handling, resolution time, etc.), capacity and utilization modeling, etc.
Provide intelligence on the correlation between engagement and retention/spend patterns
Own compensation & incentive design for the channel, including analysis and recommendations on how best to motivate service representatives
Leverage insights gleaned from analyses to proactively identify signals and inform appropriate action to address/leverage
Tailor and synthesize analyses/processes down to clear communications and presentations
Assist in developing and delivering presentations for senior leadership
